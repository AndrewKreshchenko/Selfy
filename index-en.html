About

Dana Queen

soloist of the group

Dana Queen was born in Kyiv on the 19th of July, 1991. Finalist of the show “I Want To Play VIA”. She graduated from the Kyiv Municipal Academy of Pop and Circus Arts, received a solo vocalist specialty. Later she has got a master's degree in NAKKKIM, specializing in solo vocalist, vocal instructor. Since 8, little Dana played the guitar and sang at concerts. Now she is the laureate and prizewinner of international and Ukrainian pop vocal competitions. Speakers in Paris, Berlin, Dresden, Warsaw, Prague, Kyrgyzstan. About two years he was a vocalist in the group “Trichy”. For more than a year She had been singing in the group “SMS” for more than a year. In general, she perform on a stage in Ukraine, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan and Turkey.

Таня Ли

soloist of the group

Tanya Lee was born in Vasylivka city, on the 2th of September, 1995, in the Zaporozhye region. Since childhood she carry on vocal. From 2006 to 2013 she had been studied at Fleur Variety School. In 2015 Vocal Intensive, at Lani Merkulova, was held. From 2005 to 2011 she became the winner and laureate of more than 30 regional, all-Ukrainian and international competitions and festivals of children's and youthful creativity in the nomination “Variety vocals”, and more than 70 contests and festivals in nominations of pop, folk and modern dance. In 2012, she entered the top 100 country singers in the X-Factor show, season 3.

История группы

Selfy music group was founded in January 2014 by producer, composer and performer of songs Alexander Yaremenko. The band consists of two wonderful girls Tanya Lee (Tetyana Dukhonchenko) and Dana Queen (Olga Khareba).

Girls have excellent vocal data and, in most cases, sing off their songs live. The audience was being greatly surprised herewith at one of the concerts in Kyrgyzstan. At that time the program was stopped at customer's request, after he found out that the girls had a lounge version of all their songs and they sang them off live. But offering them a guitar player, they continued to perform their songs, create an unforgettable atmosphere and surprise their foreign colleagues.

The business card of the band is the composition of "Gali baluvala". After the premiere of the song, more than 15 fan clips appeared on the Internet in the first month. The official self-made clip of Selfy group has risen in the charts of the main musical channels of Ukraine M1 and M2 in the first place for three weeks: the first place is "The Main Hit-Parade of Ukraine" and the second place "First Ukrainian". Over the first week of the premiere on the Internet, the clip was viewed by over 150,000 people, and currently more than 200,000.
