Selfy music group was formed by producer, composer and performer of songs Alexander Yaremenko in January 2014. The band consists of two wonderful girls Tanya Lee (Tetyana Dukhonchenko) and Dana Queen (Olga Khareba). The name of the group comes from «Selfmade girls», which in English means «Girls who make themselves» – self-improving and reaching their dreamed peaks.

The girls have excellent vocal skills and, in most cases, sing off their songs live. The audience was greatly surprised herewith at one of the concerts in Kyrgyzstan, when the program was stopped. Then girls were offered a guitarist to accompaniment and they continued the performance, this way made an unforgettable atmosphere and surprised their foreign colleagues.

The business card of the band is the composition of «Galya baluvala». After the song released, more than 15 fan clips appeared on the Internet in the first month. The official self-made clip of Selfy group was originally intended to appear on the main musical channels of Ukraine M1 and M2 in the first place for three weeks: the first place is «The Main Hit-Parade of Ukraine» and the second place «First Ukrainian». Over the first week of the premiere on the Internet, the clip was viewed by over 150,000 people, and currently more than 200 000.

Dana Queen

soloist of the group

Dana Queen was born in Kyiv on 19 July, 1991. Finalist of the show «I Want To Play VIA». She graduated from the Kyiv Municipal Academy of Pop and Circus Arts, has got a solo vocalist specialty. Later she has got a master's degree in NAKKKIM, specializing in solo vocalist, vocal instructor. Since 8, little Dana played the guitar and sang at concerts. Now she is a laureate and prizewinner of international and Ukrainian pop vocal competitions. She performed in Paris, Berlin, Dresden, Warsaw, Prague, Kyrgyzstan. For more than a year she had been a vocalist in the group «Trichy». Also she had been singing in the group «SMS» for more than a year. In general, she performs on stage in Ukraine, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan and Turkey.

Tanya Lee

soloist of the group

Tanya Lee was born in Vasylivka city, on 2 September, 1995, in the Zaporizhia region. Since childhood she carry on vocal. From 2006 to 2013 she had been studing at Fleur Variety School. In 2015 Tanya was trained at Lani Merkulova Vocal Intensive. From 2005 to 2011 she became the winner and a laureate of more than 30 regional, all-Ukrainian and international competitions and festivals of children's and youthful creativity in the nomination «Variety vocals», and more than 70 contests and festivals in nominations of pop, folk and modern dance. In 2012, she entered the top 100 country singers in the X-Factor show, season 3.
